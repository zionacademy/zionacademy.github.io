Tuition and

Payments

Tuition

Tuition for the school year is divided into ten equal payments, due on the 1st of each month.

Kindergarten Tuition

$900.00 per student

Grades 1-12 Tuition
  • First child per family: $1,750.00
  • Second child: $1,400.00
  • Additional children: $1,000.00

School Uniform

Students are asked to observe a dress code while attending Zion Academy. Families may purchase school uniforms at French Toast Schoolbox. This link provides access to a customized uniform selection fit for our school. Families are not required to purchase uniforms from the following site, but may find shopping easier through this service.

If accessing French Toasts Schoolbox from another link, select 'Shop by School' to begin shopping. Our selection may be found by searching 'Zion Academy' or QS5HSAM – our school code in that website.

Textbook Costs

All textbooks are purchased by your family for you to keep. High School Book Kit costs depend on the courses chosen. All Book Kits must be purchased in their entirety.

  • Kindergarten: $155.00 per student
  • Grades 1-8: $270.00 per student
  • Grades 9-12: $290.00 per student and up

Fees

Registration Fees are $75 per student, due June 1. School field trips and activities may require additional fees per student throughout the year depending on the activity.

Online Payment

Families are welcome to make online payments through their own bank to Zion Academy.
