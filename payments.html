Zion Academy

Online Payment

Zion Academy receives online payment through Dwolla. Payers set up and pay from their own Dwolla account. Payments may also be set up to recur monthly if desired. To access Dwolla, please click here.

Tuition

Kindergarten Tuition: $825.00 per student
Grades 1-12 Tuition:

  • First child per family: $1,650.00
  • Second child: $1,350.00
  • Additional children: $1,000.00

Tuition for the school year is divided into ten equal payments, due on the 1st of each month.

Textbook Costs

  • Kindergarten: $130.00 per student
  • Grades 1-8: $245.00 per student
  • Grades 9-12: $265.00 per student and up

All textbooks are purchased by your family for you to keep. High School Book Kit costs depend on the courses chosen. All Book Kits must be purchased in their entirety.

Fees

Registration Fees are $75 per student, due June 1. School field trips and activities may require additional fees per student throughout the year depending on the activity.
