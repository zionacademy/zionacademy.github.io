How to Apply

If you are interested in enrolling your child for the next school year, you will need to:

  1. Complete the Inquiry Form to let us know you are interested in applying, if possible before March 1.

  2. After receiving the Application Packet from Zion Academy, complete the Application for Enrollment form for each student applying. Send the form to Zion Academy by postal mail or deliver it by hand to our office by April 1.

  3. Provide current School Records from the current school, and from home schooling by April 1. A final Official School Record and Transcript for the current year must be on file before the first day of school at Zion Academy.

  4. Submit the $25 Family Application Fee with the completed applications for your family by April 1.

  5. Copy of the student’s Birth Certificate is required by April 1.

  6. If accepted, pay the Registration Fee by June 1.

  7. A record of all immunizations for each child is required to be on file by the first day of school.

If you have any questions about the application process, please feel free to contact us.

Our Curriculum

The A Beka DVD school is used for Zion Academy’s curriculum. Each grade and subject is taught from DVD by a master teacher from Pensacola Christian Academy, Florida. The A Beka curriculum is highly acclaimed among Christian schools and homeschools worldwide.

The A Beka Bible curriculum for all grades coupled with the godly influence of our teachers and staff are some of the ways Zion Academy strives to provide a solid Biblical foundation for its students and to assist students in developing a meaningful relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ.

In addition, Zion Academy provides teachers in our classrooms to aid the students with class work. Classes such as art, music, PE and foreign language are taught in the traditional class setting by our own Zion Academy teachers.
