How to Apply

If you are interested in enrolling your child for the next school year, you will need to:

  1. Complete and submit the Application for Enrollment form for each new student by April 1.

  2. Provide current School Records from the current school, and from home schooling by April 1. (A final Official School Record and Transcript for the current year must be on file before the first day of school at Zion Academy.)

  3. Copy of the student’s Birth Certificate is required by April 1.

  4. If accepted, pay the Registration Fee by June 1.

  5. A record of all immunizations for each child is required to be on file by the first day of school.


If you have any questions about the application process, please feel free to contact us.
