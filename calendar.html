Academic Calendar

Below you will find the school calendar, which highlights school session days, tuition deadlines, and other important dates. Please feel free to copy any event to your calendar.

Important Dates

April 1: Registration Deadline
All registrations for enrollment for new and returning students are due by April 1st.

June 1: Registration Fee
A Registration Fee of $75 is due. The fee is refunded if an applying student is not accepted into Zion Academy.

July 1: Book Payments Due
Book Kit Costs are due. Also, High School students must select their courses by this date as well.

August 1: First Tuition Payment Due
Each successive tuition payment is due on the first of each month for a total of ten months.

School Sessions

The school year begins near the end of August, and ends near the middle of June. School begins at 8:15 am and concludes at 2:45 pm, with Kindergarten concluding at 12:00 noon. Parents drop off their Grades 1-12 students by 8:10 am, and pick up by 2:50 pm.
